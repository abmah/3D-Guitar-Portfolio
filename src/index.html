College 3D Campus
A college assistant project that combines a 3D visualization of the campus with real-time stats and an AI backend server. Users can explore the campus using Blender and Three.js, while also accessing their schedules and asking college-related questions.
College Assistant AI
A Node.js application that utilizes Express and WebSocket to handle college-related questions. It leverages the power of the node-nlp library for natural language processing. The application efficiently processes incoming messages, detects the intent of the question, and delivers relevant answers.
